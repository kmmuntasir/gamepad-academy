Submarine Sonar

Creatures discovered: 0 / 16

How to play

  • Left stick — glide the submarine through the dark water.
  • L3 (click left stick) — emit a sonar ping that briefly lights up hidden fish and treasure.
  • R3 (click right stick) — cycle the submarine's headlight color.
  • Explore — every creature you reveal with a ping or glide into gets discovered. Find them all!
  • Keyboard works too: arrows / WASD to move, C to ping, V to change color.
